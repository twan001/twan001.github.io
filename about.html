About Me

Hi, my name is Thomas Wan and I am the creator of UStory.
The purpose of this website is not only to allow me to practice web-development skills,
but also to educate incoming students to any university. I do not want freshman students to go into college clueless about how to adapt to the new environment. I want my viewers of my website to learn from mistakes and experiences, before going into college.
I want the viewers of my website to learn from the mistakes
that I have made as a freshman in college and make the most out of their experience.
